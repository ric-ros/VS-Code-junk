üîß Four Technical Categories

1. üìù SYNTAX - The Grammar Police

Focus: Structure, grammar, word boundaries, grammatical functions

Key Tasks:

  • Tokenization: "Hello world!" ‚Üí ["Hello", "world", "!"]
  • POS Tagging: "I enjoy studying" ‚Üí [Pronoun, Verb, Verb]

Major Challenge: Syntactic ambiguity - same word, different grammatical roles

Example: "The duck runs fast" (verb) vs "The baseball runs scored" (noun)

2. üéØ SEMANTICS - The Meaning Finder

Focus: Understanding actual meaning and context

Connection to Unit 1: This is exactly what transformer attention mechanisms solve!

Classic Examples:

  • Bank: Financial institution vs. riverbank
  • Orange: Color vs. fruit
  • Run: Jog vs. manage vs. operate

Why AI Winter Happened: Early systems couldn't handle semantic ambiguity!

3. üí¨ DISCOURSE - The Context Master

Focus: Understanding text longer than single sentences

Capabilities:

  • Conversation flow: "I went to the bank. It was closed." (knows "it" = bank)
  • Narrative understanding: Following story plots across paragraphs
  • Context maintenance: Remembering earlier parts of conversation

Real Example: I remember our Unit 1 transformer discussion from earlier messages!

4. üó£Ô∏è SPEECH - The Sound Processor

Focus: Audio aspects - pronunciation, accents, intonation

Key Components:

  • Phonetics: How words sound
  • Prosody: Rhythm, stress, intonation
  • Accent handling: Understanding different pronunciations
  • Audio processing: Converting sound waves to text

2. üîÑ Similar Object Confusion
