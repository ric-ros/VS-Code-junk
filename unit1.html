üéØ EXAM FOCUS: Understanding Word Embeddings

üìä Word Embeddings: Step-by-Step Process

Step 1: Starting Point

"bank" begins with random vector: [0.1, 0.3, 0.8]

Step 2: Geographic Context Training

Sentence: "The river bank was muddy"

AI tries to predict: "river" given "bank"

Current prediction: "money" ‚ùå (wrong!)

Adjustment: Increase geography dimension, decrease finance dimension

New vector: [0.4, 0.3, 0.5]

Step 3: Financial Context Training

Sentence: "I deposited money at the bank"

AI tries to predict: "money" given "bank"

Current prediction: "water" ‚ùå (wrong!)

Adjustment: Increase finance dimension, adjust location dimension

New vector: [0.4, 0.6, 0.7]

Step 4: Final Result

Final vector: [0.5, 0.6, 0.7]

Meaning:

  • Dimension 1 (0.5): Geography/Nature contexts
  • Dimension 2 (0.6): Location/Place contexts
  • Dimension 3 (0.7): Finance/Institution contexts

Result: Single vector captures BOTH meanings!
