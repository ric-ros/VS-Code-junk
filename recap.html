üèõÔ∏è MARKOV DECISION PROCESS

Markov Property: Future depends only on present state, not entire history

Mathematical: P(St+1 | St) = P(St+1 | St, St-1, ...)

Component Symbol Description
States S All possible situations
Actions A All possible moves
Transition Probabilities P Probability of reaching next state
Rewards R Immediate feedback
Discount Factor Œ≥ How much future rewards matter

üéØ MODEL-FREE
